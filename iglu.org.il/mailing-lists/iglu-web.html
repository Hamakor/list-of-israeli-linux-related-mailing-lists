IGLU's Website Adminstration (IGLU-web)

Description

The administrative group of the Linux-IL website holds its discussion, announcements and reports here.

Posting Guidelines

Whatever is related to the Israeli Group of Linux Users' web-site. Other messages should be directed to the other lists.
